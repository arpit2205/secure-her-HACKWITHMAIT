Women should never feel alone while walking their way to work, home or a party. In case of some trouble or issue, she would feel secure if her family is always keeping an eye on her location.

In case of emergency,

1. Share your current location

Tap on the "SHARE LOCATION" button, then select contacts from your phonebook with whom you want to share your location. Then, simply send the automatically generated SOS message.

2. Ring an SOS Alarm

Tap on the "SOS ALERT" button to ring an emergency security alarm.
